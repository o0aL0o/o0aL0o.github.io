Man VS Alien

Introduction

Our game topic is a top view gun game. While the player is shooting a monster and killing them, he is getting an experience value in order to enhance the player levels. When upgrading his level, he could choose some new weapon or strengthening such as adding a heart or increase the attack. Also, the game is a level game. The player should pass each level and each level will increase with the number of passes.


Control

WASD to move
SPACE BAR to shoot
F to interact object


Playthrough

when the player gains enough exp. Player will level up. When level up players will gain 3 Upgrade cards to choose one use.
Upgrade card:
1.upgrade HP to player
2. upgrade damage to player
3. add running speed
4. new a lighting ball attack. when lighting ball hit level 3 will add a new one
