學好廣東話 Learn Cantonese well

Introduction

This is a mobile app that helps people learn Cantonese. It is designed for people who are not familiar with Cantonese. The app provides a simple and easy way to learn Cantonese. It includes a variety of features, such as vocabulary learning, quizzes, and watching videos. To add intersting features, after user finish a quiz or vocabulary learning. They will earn coins. And this coins can purchase some items in the shop. Each as customization for your AI. User can buy hat, clothes, face or color. To add more interaction, user can use the pofile system. User can make friends, check how many task they have done, and how many coins they have.
