BattleTank

Introduction

The game flow of our game is to kill monsters, gain money, build upgrades, and kill stronger monsters.

Movement:


The user is controlling a tank using WASD. However, moving a tank is different from controlling a player. And the movement controller script is different. The player can use “W” and “S” to control forward and backward. “A” and “D” control the rotation left and right. And for the movement, also added acceleration and deceleration to let the movement feel better.

Build mode:

Pressing “B” will enter build mode. The game will pause. Depending on how much money the player has, they player can decide what weapon to build on their tank. And using 1,2,3 number keys to switch to different weapons to build. The amount of weapons is unlimited, and the combination is infinite.

Weapon:

Machine gun, cannon, and wall. They all have different attack styles and health points.

Enemy spawn event: The enemy is spawned around the tank with a range. It also has a timer for counting which enemy to spawn. In the first minute, it spawns a zombie. The next minute will spawn a vampire. The next minute will spawn an egg monster. Three minutes later, it will increase the number of enemy spawns. The difficulty will increase when time passes.

Enemy:

Each enemy has its own move and attack. There are long-range attacks and close-range attacks.
