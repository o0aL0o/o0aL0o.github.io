Homecoming

Introduction

"Homecoming" is an immersive installation that explores the multifaceted concept of home through the lens of cultural diversity. Through a combination of physical elements, interactive triggers, and projected visuals, the installation invites viewers to contemplate their sense of belonging and identity in a globalized world by incorporating the origins of our diverse group members, representing Hong Kong, Indonesia, Bangladesh, and Germany. The installation aims to evoke a sense of shared experiences and highlight the complexities of defining "home" for individuals with multiple cultural influences. Through this exploration, "Homecoming" sparks meaningful conversations about cultural identity, connection, the universal longing for a place to call home, and how different experiences may invoke this sense of being an outsider.
