Mini game

Introduction

Create a game using a webcam to track a blue circle. First, create a scoreboard on the screen. Then, In the webcam, make a random position of a small green circle every 3 seconds. When detecting a blue circle is close enough or there is collision within the circle, remove the circle with collision within and add one point to the board. Also, create three random positions of small red rectangles in the webcam. When detect a blue circle is close enough or collision within the rectangle, stop the webcam, print "Finish" and print the final score.
