JungleJumper

Introduction

Before I had the final idea, I wanted to create a cyberpunk-feel city, but I did not find perfect access, and the amount of access was insufficient for me to make a level. Then, after I searched from in the fab, I downloaded a ton of access to jungle, trees, water, rocks, etc. And it is perfect for me to create a jungle theme.

When creating the level, My first idea was to make a floating island. Under it are the sky and clouds. But the problem is the feel is the view is not interesting. So, I changed my idea to a typical island surrounded by water and a larger island. Because I had the floating island idea before, I added some floating rocks in the sky and created a magical theme. To get things started, building a village at the beginning is an excellent idea because a great adventure always starts in a village. Then, I wanted to create an adventure for the player, so I added different islands/areas for the player to travel. To travel to other places, players need to complete the challenge. So, I added parkour, maze, and finding Key.
